You got: London!

London, the vibrant capital of England, is a bustling metropolis known for its rich history and diverse culture. Spanning the River Thames, the city seamlessly blends the old with the new, showcasing iconic landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the modern Shard skyscraper. A hub for arts, fashion, and finance, London boasts an array of world-id museums, theaters, and restaurants. Its multicultural neighborhoods, from the historic streets of Covent Garden to the upscale charm of Notting Hill, reflect a dynamic tapestry of global influences, making London a captivating destination for visitors and residents alike.
